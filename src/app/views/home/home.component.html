DakotaFox

As the cofounder of DakotaFox, we designed a physical product for rural roads in North Dakota to alert truck drivers to low hanging power lines. Accidents created from power line collisions were costing companies around $50,000 USD.

After conducting numerous interviews, rapid prototyping and field testing, we built a solution that stood up to the unique challenges of the extreme North Dakota weather and improved people’s safety.
